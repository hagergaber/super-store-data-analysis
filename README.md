# 📊 Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is part of my learning journey in Data Analytics using Power BI.

The goal of this analysis is to explore the relationship between **Sales, Profit, and Discount**, and understand how business decisions (like discounting) impact overall profitability.

---

## 📁 Dataset

* Superstore dataset (2011–2015)
* Includes: Sales, Profit, Discount, Category, Country, Order Date, etc.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze sales performance over time
* Identify top-performing countries and categories
* Understand the relationship between discount and profit
* Evaluate overall business profitability

---

## 📊 Dashboards

### 1. Sales Dashboard

* Total Sales and Total Orders
* Sales trend over time
* Top 5 countries by sales
* Sales distribution by segment and category

### 2. Profit Dashboard

* Total Profit and Profit Margin
* Top 5 categories by profit
* Sales vs Profit trend over time
* Discount vs Profit relationship (Scatter Plot)

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Sales and profit both show a steady growth over time
* Higher discounts are associated with lower profit
* Business growth is partially driven by discount strategies
* Profit margin (~11%) indicates room for improvement

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Optimize discount strategies to improve profitability
* Focus on high-performing categories like Technology
* Expand into new markets to reduce dependency on top regions

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

* Power BI
* Power Query (Data Cleaning)
* DAX (for calculations like Profit Margin)
